✅ Do’s
Think before you post – consider how it may affect your reputation.
Maintain privacy settings – regularly review who can see your content.
Share authentic and respectful content – be mindful of others.
Verify information before sharing – avoid spreading misinformation.
Balance online and offline life – manage screen time effectively.
❌ Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, passwords, etc.).
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content.
Don’t trust or click on suspicious links.
Don’t overshare every aspect of your personal life.
